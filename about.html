Observing the nature of life always inspires me. I am in my element when I attune to the ebbs and flows of a subject. I curate from every subject, animate to inanimate subjects - from singular to multiple subjects; whether of an individual subject or of a multitude at a gathering of people or nature’s event. Life happening in its fundamentals is my plain canvas… and I love to find a way to curate subjects into a visual narrative

The most elemental aspect of photography is people. Even when there is no human in the image, a picture is designed to engage someone’s eye. People are focal and it’s light that captures them... I use the definitive light of a subject to understand and communicate life with the lens of a camera.
